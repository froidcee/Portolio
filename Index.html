The Short Version

I Don't Wait For Trends To Land

Most marketers wait for trends to land. I've never been wired that way. Over 8+ years in performance marketing, including 4+ years in Amazon advertising, I've owned and scaled growth for 500+ accounts across NA, EU and APAC, partnering with both emerging brands and established sellers.

I began on the inside at Amazon, working directly with advertisers and seeing firsthand how top-performing (and underperforming) accounts are built and managed. I don't just live at campaign level - I look at how the market is moving, how demand is shifting, and where we're leaving money on the table, then adapt structure and creative storytelling around that.

In most of the accounts I've handled, this approach has improved ROAS by uncovering missed opportunities and aligning campaigns with real market dynamics, rather than just pushing buttons in the ad console.

"Invent and simplify" is my core strength. I take messy, noisy performance data and turn it into clean, simple decisions teams can actually execute. People tell me I make complex performance topics easy to understand - I work that muscle on purpose, because the smartest strategy means nothing if the person across the table doesn't really get it.

If you'd benefit from expert guidance on brand growth, or want to unpack a complex performance challenge, I'm always open to a thoughtful conversation.
